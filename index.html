About Soccer Bros

Soccer Bros 2 is the sequel to the original Soccer Bros game! It's a simple, fast-paced soccer game with all kinds of crazy action! With online multiplayer gameplay and tons of futbol mayhem!

Use either the arrow keys or WASD to control your bro. Space bar will shoot, pass, and tackle!

FAQ

Why isn't the game loading?

You should check your internet connection and make sure our site isn't blocked. Also, disable all ad blockers.

How do I play?

Use your arrow keys and put the ball into the net!

Which browser should I use?

You should use Chrome. It is the only browser guaranteed to work. Soccer Bros has also been tested to work on Firefox and Safari, but not guaranteed.
